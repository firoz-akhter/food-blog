Ricotta Berry French Toast Casserole

Welcome to a breakfast and brunch favorite that’s sure to impress: Ricotta Berry French Toast Casserole! This delightful dish combines the rich, creamy goodness of ricotta cheese with the sweet and tart flavors of fresh berries, all layered over soft, custardy French toast. It’s an easy, make-ahead recipe that’s perfect for weekend mornings, holiday brunches, or any special occasion.

  • Make-Ahead Convenience: Prepare the night before and bake in the morning.
  • Versatile: Customize with your favorite fruits or add nuts for extra crunch.
  • Perfect for Crowds: Ideal for family breakfasts or brunch gatherings.
  • Fresh Berries: Provides bursts of flavor and color.
  • Bread: French bread or brioche for texture.
  • Egg Mixture: Eggs, milk, vanilla for base.
  • Ricotta Filling: Ricotta cheese with honey.
  • Bake: Bake at 175°C for 45-50 minutes.

Swedish Pancakes

Welcome to a delicious journey into the world of Swedish Pancakes, a classic Scandinavian breakfast treat! Known for their thin, delicate texture and slightly sweet flavor, Swedish pancakes (or "Pannkakor") are a delightful change from traditional American pancakes. Perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even dessert, these pancakes are sure to become a favorite in your home.

  • Slightly Sweet Flavor: Sweetness makes them versatile for sweet and savory toppings.
  • Kid-Friendly: Fun to make and eat, Swedish pancakes are liked by children and adults.
  • Thin and Delicate: Unlike traditional pancakes, Swedish pancakes are thin and light.
  • Versatile Serving Options: Enjoy with lingonberry jam, fresh fruits, cream, or sugar.
  • Flour: Flour forms the base of the batter.
  • Eggs: Provide structure and richness.
  • Butter: Adds flavor, achieve a brown finish.
  • Milk: Gives thin and smooth consistency.
